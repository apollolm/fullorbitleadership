Ryan Whitley

Co-Founder & Executive Coach

Ryan is a Senior Software Engineering Manager at Planet, the leading provider of global, near-daily satellite imagery data and insights. With over 20 years of experience, he has also led cross-functional data and software engineering teams at software companies like Tableau and Salesforce.

Throughout his career, he has had the privilege of working with renowned organizations such as NASA, The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, and The American Red Cross, among others, delivering high-quality geospatial solutions that enable data visualization, analysis, and decision-making. As a leader, he prioritizes the growth and development of his teams, fostering an inclusive environment that encourages continuous learning and innovation.

Ryan is passionate about promoting diversity and inclusion in the tech industry. He has founded internal coaching practices at both Planet and Salesforce, focusing on supporting underrepresented employees as well as mentoring aspiring technologists of color.

Sarah Mitchell Parker

Co-Founder & Leadership Strategist

With over two decades of experience in the aerospace industry, Sarah Mitchell Parker now serves as the Director of Technical Program Management (TPM) at Saildrone. Her transition from space to ocean exploration has proven exhilarating, showcasing her adaptability and passion for cutting-edge technologies.

Throughout her career, Sarah has consistently demonstrated a remarkable ability to bring out the best in people. This natural talent led her to pursue coaching, where she found a perfect synergy between her leadership skills and desire to help others excel. Sarah firmly believes that while technical challenges may be similar across projects, it's the people who truly differentiate successful outcomes.

Drawing from 15+ years in management roles, Sarah has collaborated with professionals at all levels, from entry-level positions to C-suite executives. These diverse experiences have sharpened her emotional intelligence and deepened her understanding of the unique challenges within the tech industry.

To formalize her coaching expertise, Sarah completed training from the Center for Creative Leadership (CCL) and is currently working toward her International Coach Federation (ICF) certification.

Sarah has a BSE in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Michigan, Dual MSE in Engineering Management / MBA from San Jose State University, and is a Certified Yoga instructor.

Vandhana Lal

Partner & Executive Mentor

Vandhana is a mentor and an aspiring leadership coach passionate about empowering individuals to discover their potential and achieve their career aspirations. With a strong foundation and commitment to lifelong learning, Vandhana is dedicated to guiding others through career transitions, personal development challenges, and professional growth opportunities.

As an aspiring leadership coach, Vandhana is eager to leverage her ongoing coaching training to make a meaningful impact on individuals' lives. She is committed to helping clients navigate career choices, enhance their professional skills, and achieve personal fulfillment in their chosen fields.
